What is TruAuth?

TruAuth is a blockchain-powered platform ensuring product authenticity for premium nutrition and nutraceuticals. Using tamper-proof QR/NFC tags, every supply chain step is immutably logged on a blockchain, allowing instant verification of a product's origin and genuineness to combat counterfeiting and tampering.

Crucially, TruAuth also independently verifies key product claims (e.g., "organic," "clinically tested") through certified labs and third-party audits, linking verifiable proof directly to the product's digital identity. This dual focus aims to establish TruAuth as the industry's trusted standard.
